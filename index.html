Top 4 Travel Destinations in 2024

Here is a list of my top 4 travel destinations for 2024! It has been an extraordinary year where I travelled across more cities and discovered wonderful gems around the world. Read along to find a travel location that speaks to you!

Skyline of Doha, Qatar 2024.

The mesmerising views of Doha's skyline at night illuminated my travel in the Middle East. Read more about this exciting trip:

2. Thailand, Southeast Asia

Doi Inthanon, Ban Luang, Thailand 2024.

Catching the sunsets in the valleys of Dai Intahanon, Northern Thailand was the highlight of my trip to Southeast Asia. Meeting the locals filled with hospitality and positivity was something which I truly treasure about visiting Thailand! Read more about this exciting trip:

Badshahi Mosque, Lahore, Pakistan 2024.

Machu Picchu, Peru 2024.

Machu Picchu is one of the best archeological sites to visit. It's an incredible remain of the Inca Civilisation and learning about its history was a spectacular experience. Read more about this exciting trip:
